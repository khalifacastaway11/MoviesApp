## 🎬 Welcome to Our Cinema Application

We are excited to unveil our latest application that brings the world of cinema right to your fingertips. This application leverages the power of the TMDB (The Movie Database) API to provide a seamless and intuitive experience for film enthusiasts. Whether you're looking to discover new movies or dive deep into the details of your favorites, our app has got you covered.

### 🌟 Key Features:

#### 🔍 Comprehensive Film Search:
- Easily search for movies by title.
- Filter through a vast database to find exactly what you're looking for.

#### 📜 Detailed Film Information:
- Access extensive details about each movie including title, release date, director, genre, and an overview.
- View the movie poster to get a visual representation.

#### 🎥 Director and Cast Information:
- Quickly identify the director of the film.
- Discover the top actors and actresses involved in the movie.

#### 🎭 Actor Search within Film Details:
- Filter through the cast to find specific actors with ease.
